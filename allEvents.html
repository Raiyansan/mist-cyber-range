INDEPENDENCE DAY PROGRAMMING CONTEST-2022 ORGANIZED BY MIST COMPUTER CLUB

To celebrate our glorious golden jubilee of independence, Dept of CSE, MIST is organizing ICT innovation fest for the university students of Bangladesh to share their innovative solutions to problems we face as a nation today. In the Hackathon, Project Showcasing, and Mobile app contest, students will get to produce innovative solutions, sharpen their technical skills, and win cumulative prize money of BDT 315,000. Be sure to check the event pages for details and submit your ideas by the given deadline!
